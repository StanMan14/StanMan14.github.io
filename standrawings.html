Art

I specialise in Portraits/ Remixing portraits (either face, mid body or full body)
The drwaings will be in graphite pencil and charcoal.
If you want a drawing contact me on email. Click here --> @StanMan14
NOTE* All payments will be made through PayPal and the prices range from the picture.
A maximum of 2 people will be able to order and I will notify if the list is full.
Lastly, PLEASE don't send me complicated things to draw.

Instructions

1. Contact me on email.
2. Tell me exactly what you want and give me a picture eg. Your selfie.
NOTE* If the picture you want is revealing and you really want this, you will be required to sign a contract of consent provided by me.
3. When I am done, I will photocopy the drawing to you and you can either print it out or keep it on your device BUT before that, you will be required to pay before I can give you the picture.
4. Enjoy!
